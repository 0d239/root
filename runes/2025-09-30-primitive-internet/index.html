ode to the primitive internet

2025‑09‑30

The old web wasn’t pretty, but it was personal. View‑source was a love letter. This site tries to keep a little of that energy alive.

Blurbs are just CSS files with content hidden in variables. Tracks ship with tiny manifests. The rest is HTML. That’s the joke. And the point.

rules of the garden
