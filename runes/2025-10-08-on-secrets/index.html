on secrets

2025-10-08

i don't really like secrets much. heavily case dependent you could argue. but in general i think the truth likely thrives with room to swim.

nonetheless, lately it's funny because i feel like i have a pretty big secret.

i know in my bones that these songs are something.

i've been around blood long enough to be familiar. i know my way around a crate or two. i'm the first critique and the last praise. and even i know the truth.

i know i'm not crazy like i know the sun goes down every evening and my huelo loves my huela every morning.

i don't need to change the world or anything.
it is only freedom i have ever sought.
sometimes the evidence wants to convince me that this freedom is en route.
that all this blood might help me breathe.

it is 12:23 am right now and i'm listening to my secret symphony. one day it will be the worlds. 24 songs done. 4 other near completion. trimming down to 23. inshallah maktub.
