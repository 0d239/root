on sacred mania

2025-10-03

with strong lines of logic to support both sides, i still find myself evaded by a confident stance on whether or not the act of creation is a natural art.

on one hand, it is the most normal form of movement i have ever known. but on another, at times it makes me lose my mind.

some progressively, possibly knowns:

  • to create is to summon something that is not with you
  • ^proficiency in such a task likely has an inverse relationship with presence and validation in some provable but ranged fashion
  • the only way to reach the moon is to leave the earth
  • the only way back to earth is to leave the moon

    • some likely unknowns:

  • nobody can be certain if a "normal" exists, and it is not too efficient to consider
  • are the nfl's parity systems strong enough? or do they measure context on a window larger than common intuition would label practictal? i am not too sure. but hopefully the raiders improve
