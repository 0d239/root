on sacred mania

2025-10-03

i still find myself evaded by a confident stance on the following:

  • what is normal?
  • what is natural?
  • whether either of the aforementioned things exists
  • whether either of the aforementioned things matters
  • whether or not there is any difference betweem them.

    • a few knowns:

  • to create is to summon something that is not with you
  • ^proficiency in such a task likely has an inverse relationship with presence and validation in some provable but ranged fashion
  • the only way to reach the moon is to leave the earth
  • the only way back to earth is to leave the moon

    • some more unknowns:

  • are the nfl's parity systems enough? would their context window size benefit from calibration? #raiders
  • for how long can my spirit undergo alchemy?
  • how many times has will the phoenix be born?

    • some comforts:

  • definition demands the loneliness of the frontier. the last element cannot have two neighbors.
  • documentation of those before you can be a great ailment to treat an absence of reflection
  • it is not normal to be able to do anything in this world. so the fact that i know i can do this means i am not normal. and i need to be okay with not being normal so that it doesn't scare me later when i get more feedback that reasserts this signal.
  • the harmonies of madness and greatness have long helped provide a stable reference point for the concept of symphony.
  • as long as i get up and walk every single day, i have to end up somewhere.
